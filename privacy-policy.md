# Privacy Policy for Home Automation SMS

**Effective Date:** April 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our household Home Automation SMS service (the “Service”).

## What this Service is

The Service allows approved household users to send text commands (for example, status or light controls) and receive text responses related to those commands.  
This Service is **not** used for marketing or promotional campaigns.

## Information we collect

When you use the Service, we may collect:

- Your mobile phone number
- Message content you send and receive
- Date/time of messages
- Technical metadata needed for delivery, fraud prevention, and troubleshooting

## How we use information

We use collected information only to:

- Verify authorized users
- Process and respond to home automation commands
- Enforce safety controls (such as confirmation for sensitive actions)
- Maintain logs for reliability, security, and audit purposes

## Sharing of information

- We do **not** sell your personal information.
- We do **not** share mobile information with third parties for marketing purposes.
- Information is shared only with service providers required to operate the Service (for example, SMS delivery platforms) and only as needed.

## Data retention

We retain message and related log data only as long as needed for operation, security, troubleshooting, and compliance, then delete or anonymize it when no longer required.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical controls to protect data, including access controls and secret management practices. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your choices

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying **STOP**.  
You can request help by replying **HELP**.

## Children’s privacy

This Service is intended for household use and is not directed to children under 13 without parent/guardian supervision.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
